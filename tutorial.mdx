## Mint an Inscription

If you want to mint a corresponding Suirc20 inscription, move to the “Inscribe” page and select the “Mint” tag. Enter the tick of the inscription you would like to mint, enter the amount you want for a single mint (can’t exceed the limit per mint for this particular inscription) and how many mints you want to repeat. Currently you can repeat up to 10 mints each time.

![img](https://lh7-us.googleusercontent.com/o9QQnTIljW_qwaLZ4rLk_8iZc0yYe9GhEETpBarqqemRe4u2FXyofE-4K7mTZ4wDl4J7vDwBJxuqrZTTJ9zSyO8IuhaeA-4yl7PB9NRd6LY2rxXfTIvu710-b4oGS-vW03uD7vcXbvEifxKXVfQSGQ)

After entering all the inputs, click “Next” to call your wallet to proceed. You need to approve corresponding times for minting transactions in your wallet just as the “Repeat” times you selected. After you approve all the transactions, your minting request will be fully completed.

![img](https://lh7-us.googleusercontent.com/7K9fnoUQYJ-54D6wCQKgUFrU3EsAVOvBM3YDoBSJxvVai5qcuucNGQ8WQ_4fy-dBr30jtuWpptnGQLg10OeozAnAyb7bnpN8kGn-pp4_rb_jJ7DAh96XIfurNEOuqHYUw6Gfyd5JZkDtT6E5iSJerw)

## Transfer Inscriptions

You may want to transfer your Suirc20 inscriptions to others. Just simply switch to the “Transfer” tag, enter the inscription tick and the amount you want to transfer, and then enter the recipient wallet address.

After confirming the inputs, click “Next” to approve your transfer request in your wallet.

![img](https://lh7-us.googleusercontent.com/v_x8d59fP0a7Aaj2wT4EuF9xxigIdYWCuXmgUmlngIsHqMMqarUTSuYTrYGKiaiNpP9do3z2p_zqlg-I_VpJihUI8C2WxCSwPobIt9WHmnKilSaExrCnbkGWsNrRB7uLLWqtfa47Ar4xaT2ZMPjW9A)

## View Balance

You can check your inscription balance under the “Balance” page. As more ecosystem wallet integrates the Suirc20 standard, you will be able to view your inscription balance in wallet directly soon.

![img](https://lh7-us.googleusercontent.com/GINTyY_Dq0NZ6qY3qFrnFps7oc1QmR1X29dTk5DJrnhKJeCxM0c4Dkng5oXWHg8pSYNoBa6XgVH_9islUqLJsmEouFLiKfZ6UP6zvMUPM81FFtInCj8eMQ7uOPpYbcNkpA0jM1ZFGgUZ1SOs-jYfeg)

## Deploy a New Inscription

If you want to deploy a brand new Suirc20 inscription. You can switch the tag to “Deploy” under the “Inscribe” page. Set a unique Tick name for the new inscription (can’t be as same as existing ones, which will be rejected). And then set the Total Supply and Limit Per Mint for your inscription.

Click “Next” to execute your deploy request.

![img](https://lh7-us.googleusercontent.com/7eITytQs9M6sLYgXaj1sLpEMDVvU2F5ibPShM4gup_FcL1fUSP5LcexyWqKjmMLdRyAbnowvfnHsw9JY3tZosyyeHL61uua3yJJC_CEACcj4mrZDsgo0rELrnQb7DW8OzxiQkvR3trUNbCU3WTNOSA)

Currently, it only supports the deployment of basic Suirc20 inscriptions. The text input, image input and other media input will be supported soon, as well as the Inscription Marketplace. Please stay tuned.
